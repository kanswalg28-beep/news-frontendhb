The Honestly Biased Collective is our founding membership — readers who don't just consume independent media, but own it.

Why the Collective Exists

Most "reader-funded" outlets still have a single owner, a board of directors, or a venture capitalist with a seat at the table. The Collective is different: it is a legal trust structure where founding members hold beneficial ownership. No billionaire. No corporate parent. No exit strategy.

When you join, you become a beneficiary of the Honestly Biased Digital Media Trust. Your contribution isn't a donation — it's an equity-equivalent stake in an institution designed to outlive its founders.

Founding Member Benefits

📊

Real-time access to financial dashboards, editorial decision logs, and AI audit trails. No "trust us" — verify yourself.

Legal Protection
